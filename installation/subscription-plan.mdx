- **Free Trial**: 15 free syncs to try the integration with basic data sync features between HubSpot and e-conomic.﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
- **Basic**: Priced at DKK 99/month, it offers unlimited sync and financial updates for customer data from e-conomic.
- **Premium**: At DKK 199/month, this plan includes comprehensive sync for customers, products, and invoices, along with financial updates and replay functionality for HubSpot activity logs.
- **Custom**: This plan is tailored to individual business needs and provides a bespoke integration solution with dedicated support, priced on a quote basis.
